## Selection priority (when comparing candidates)

Use this order — **Evidence Confidence first**:

| Priority | Criterion | Why |
|:--------:|-----------|-----|
| 1 | **Evidence Confidence** | Phase 4 needs valid evidence, not ambition |
| 2 | **Relevant** | Wrong use case → wrong proof |
| 3 | **Reachable** | Faster credible path beats perfect plan |
| 4 | **Real** | Must satisfy external-org boundary |
| 5 | **Repeatable** | Matters for scale after proof |

> A small pilot that **almost certainly succeeds** beats a large pilot that may fail before producing data.
