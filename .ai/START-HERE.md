## Forbidden

AI Brain does NOT contain:

- ❌ Planner / Executor
- ❌ Workflow Engine
- ❌ Reasoning Engine
- ❌ Autonomous Loop
- ❌ Tool Orchestrator

These belong to external agent systems.
